Triangular Board Signs

Triangular Board Signs are called Warning Signs. Warning signs or cautionary signs give information to the driver about the impending road condition.
They advice the driver to obey the rules. These signs are meant for the own safety of drivers. They call for extra vigilance from the part of drivers.
The color convention used for this type of signs is that the legend will be black in color with a white background. The shape used is upward triangular or diamond shape with red borders.
